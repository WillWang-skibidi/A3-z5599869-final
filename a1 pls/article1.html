Politicans begin to lobby for military advancement

By Yama Takemura

It is clear to many that the government only loves renewable and sustainable energy when it has applications to their military industrial complex. Whether it be nuclear powered submarines or something else, it didn't take long for the military to recognise the many potiential possibilites the BIOPODS provided in weaponised utilities.

Now we hear more and more about how the age old idea of mechanised suits of armour replacing the role of tanks and biopods would only serve to bring about this overturning. With the dangers of Biopods, like the nuclear submarine it would greatly extend the duration in which armour and its pilot would be operational before refueling, allowing them to massacre and wreak havoc for god knows how long.

While current details and leaks are limited, more updates will follow as the story develops.
